How to read your stats

A quick guide to the labels and card states you see in your profile.

Review ratings

Again

You did not remember the card. It should come back soon.

Hard

You remembered it with difficulty. The card likely needs more practice.

Good

You remembered it correctly. This is a solid successful review.

Easy

You remembered it very easily. The interval can grow more confidently.

Card states

Learning

The card is still new or not yet stable.

Review

The card is in regular long-term spaced repetition.

Relearning

The card was forgotten and is being learned again.

Due now

Cards that are ready to be reviewed at this moment.
